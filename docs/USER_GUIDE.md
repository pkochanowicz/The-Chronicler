## Your First Steps: Joining the Guild

*Before you can inscribe your legend, you must first earn a title within our order...*

### Required Guild Titles (Roles)

To register a character, you must hold one of these sacred titles:

- üåü **Wanderer** - New souls who have just begun their journey
- üó∫Ô∏è **Seeker** - Explorers who have proven their dedication

**Don't have a title?** The Chronicle will gently inform you: *"Only guild members may inscribe their tales in the archives."* Speak to a guild officer to receive your Wanderer title and begin your journey!

### What You Must Prepare

Before invoking the registration ritual, gather these components:

1. **Your Character's Full Name** - As it appears in World of Warcraft
2. **Your Character's Race** - From 11 defined bloodlines (see list below)
3. **Your Character's Class** - Must be one of the 9 Classic WoW classes
4. **Your Character's Roles** - Select 1 or more (Tank, Healer, DPS, Support)
5. **Your Character's Professions** - Optional, but enhances your legend
6. **A Portrait Image** - Your character's visage (URL required) (Optional)
7. **An Epic Backstory** - Where they came from, what shaped them (Mandatory, max 1024 characters)
8. **Personality Traits** - What defines your hero's visible nature (Optional, max 1024 characters)
9. **Memorable Quotes** - Up to 3 iconic lines (optional but recommended)

This will not only serve us at crafting a Character Sheet for you but also is compatible with TTRP addon,
which permitts in-game viewing of other players character sheets and showing them your own.
(https://github.com/tempranova/turtlerp/releases/tag/1.1.1)

### The Portrait Mystery: Obtaining Your Character's Visage

*Ah yes, the portrait‚Äîthe trickiest component! Fear not, for I shall guide you...*

**Method 1: Upload to Discord, Copy Link**
1. Go to any Discord channel where you can post messages (even a DM to yourself works!)
2. Upload your portrait image (drag & drop or click the + button)
3. Once uploaded, right-click the image ‚Üí **Copy Link**
4. Paste this URL during the registration flow

**Method 2: Use Imgur (Free Image Hosting)**
1. Visit [imgur.com](https://imgur.com)
2. Click "New post" and upload your image
3. After upload, right-click image ‚Üí **Copy image address**
4. Use this URL in registration

**Method 3: Request Default Placeholder**
- During Step 11 (The Visage), you can select "Use default placeholder"
- The Chronicle will use a mystical fallback image
- You can update it later!

**Method 4: Request AI Generation (Phase 2 Feature)**
- Coming soon! You'll be able to request an AI-generated portrait
- Officers will generate it using Stable Diffusion XL
- The Chronicle will flag your submission with `request_sdxl=TRUE`

**‚ö†Ô∏è Critical: The URL must start with `http://` or `https://`**

Invalid examples:
- ‚ùå `www.myimage.com/pic.jpg` (missing https://)
- ‚ùå Google Drive "share" links (not direct image URLs)
- ‚ùå Instagram profile URLs (not direct to image file)

## Understanding the Fates: Character States

*Let me explain the three fates that may befall your character...*

### 1. PENDING ‚Üí The Waiting Period

**What it means:** Your character has been inscribed and is awaiting judgment by the officers.

**Where you are:**
- ‚úÖ Recorded in Google Sheets with `status=PENDING` and `confirmation=TRUE`

**What officers see:**
- Your full character sheet embeds
- ‚úÖ and ‚ùå reaction options

**Next steps:**
- Officers react ‚úÖ to approve or ‚ùå to reject
- You receive a DM either way
- Average wait time: 1-2 days

### 2. REGISTERED ‚Üí Active Hero

**What it means:** An officer determined your submission wasn't ready.

## Common Mysteries Solved: Troubleshooting

*Even the mightiest magic encounters gremlins. Here's how to banish them...*

### "I can't see the /register_character command!"

**Possible Causes:**

1. **You don't have a guild role**
   - The command exists, but Discord hides it if you lack permissions
   - **Solution:** Ask an officer for the Wanderer role

2. **The bot is offline**
   - Check if "The Chronicler" (or bot name) appears offline in member list
   - **Solution:** Alert a guild administrator

3. **Slash commands haven't synced**
   - Discord can take up to 1 hour to sync new commands
   - **Solution:** Wait, or ask an admin to restart the bot

---

### "The interactive flow timed out!"

**What happened:** You took longer than 5 minutes (300 seconds) to complete a step.

**Solution:**
- Start over with `/register_character`
- The flow timer resets at each step, so you have 5 minutes PER STEP
- If you need more time, ask an officer to increase `INTERACTIVE_TIMEOUT_SECONDS`

---

### "I made a typo in my character's name!"

**Can it be fixed?** Yes, but not through the bot.

**Solution:**
1. Contact an officer
2. Officer edits the Google Sheets row directly
3. Changes sync to Discord automatically (via webhooks)

**Future:** Phase 2 will add an `/edit_character` command for self-service edits.

---

### "My portrait isn't showing!"

**Possible Causes:**

1. **Invalid URL**
   - URL doesn't start with `http://` or `https://`
   - URL is a "share link" not a direct image link
   - **Solution:** Re-upload to Imgur or Discord, get direct image URL

2. **Image host blocked**
   - Some image hosts are blocked by Discord
   - **Solution:** Use Imgur.com or Discord CDN (upload to Discord, copy link)

3. **URL broke after submission**
   - Image was deleted from host
   - **Solution:** Contact officer, provide new URL, they'll update sheets

---

### "I got rejected but don't know why!"

**Solution:**
1. Check your DM from the Chronicler‚Äîit includes the reviewing officer's Discord ID
2. Contact that officer directly (right-click their name ‚Üí Message)
3. Ask for specific feedback
4. Common issues:
   - Backstory too short (needs at least 2-3 sentences)
   - Generic personality ("nice guy" isn't enough!)
   - Portrait inappropriate
   - Lore-breaking elements

---

### "The bot said my professions are invalid!"

**What happened:** You selected more than 2 main professions or more than 6 total.

**WoW Profession Limits:**
- **2 main professions** (Alchemy, Blacksmithing, Enchanting, Engineering, Herbalism, Leatherworking, Mining, Skinning, Tailoring, Jewelcrafting)
- **4 secondary professions** (First Aid, Cooking, Fishing, Survival)

**Valid combinations:**
- ‚úÖ Mining + Blacksmithing (2 main)
- ‚úÖ Mining + Blacksmithing + Cooking + Fishing (2 main + 2 secondary)
- ‚ùå Mining + Blacksmithing + Herbalism (3 main professions - INVALID)

**Solution:** Reconsider your profession choices to match WoW rules.

**What happened:** An officer performed `/bury` on your character.

**Possible reasons:**
- You left the guild
- Your character was retired IC

**Solution:**

  1. Updating status in Google Sheets back to REGISTERED

## Tips for Epic Character Sheets

*Listen well, for these are the secrets of legendary inscriptions...*

### 1. Write a COMPELLING Backstory

**Bad backstory:**
> "Bob is a warrior. He likes fighting."

**Good backstory:**
> "Born in Stormwind's orphanage after his parents fell to the Scourge, Marcus learned to fight in the streets. When he came of age, he enlisted in the Stormwind Guard, vowing no child would suffer as he did. Now he wields his blade not for glory, but for protection‚Äîthe shield between darkness and the innocent."

**Tips:**
- Include WHERE they're from
- Include WHAT shaped them (trauma, mentor, event)
- Include WHY they adventure (motivation)
- Use vivid language ("forged in battle" not "good at fighting")

---

### 2. Make Personality SPECIFIC

**Bad personality:**
> "Nice, friendly, brave"

**Good personality:**
> "Gruff exterior masking deep compassion. Slow to trust but fiercely loyal once earned. Prone to gallows humor in dark moments. Respects strength but admires wisdom more."

**Tips:**
- Avoid generic adjectives
- Include contradictions (makes characters interesting!)
- Think about HOW they act, not just WHAT they are
- Reference their mannerisms

---

### 3. Choose MEMORABLE Quotes

**Bad quotes:**
> "Let's go!" | "Yeah!" | "Cool"

**Good quotes:**
> "My ancestors smile upon me. Can you say the same?" | "Death smiles at us all. All we can do is smile back." | "They will sing songs of this day... or they won't. Either way, I'm thirsty."

**Tips:**
- Make them sound like YOUR character (not generic lines)
- Include battle cries, catchphrases, or philosophical musings
- Reference their backstory or personality
- Use archaic language if appropriate for race/class

---

### 4. Match Portrait to Character

**Bad portrait matching:**
- Playing a scarred veteran ‚Üí Using art of a clean-faced teenager
- Playing a holy priest ‚Üí Using art of a demonic warlock

**Good portrait matching:**
- Grizzled dwarf warrior ‚Üí Portrait with scars, beard, weathered face
- Young night elf priestess ‚Üí Portrait with elegant features, moonlight aesthetic
- Undead rogue ‚Üí Portrait emphasizing skeletal features, dark colors

**Where to find portraits:**
- [ArtStation](https://www.artstation.com) - Search "fantasy character art"
- [DeviantArt](https://www.deviantart.com) - Tons of character portraits
- [Pinterest](https://www.pinterest.com) - Search "D&D character portrait"
- Commission an artist! (Support artists!)

---

### 5. Use the Three Traits Wisely

**Bad traits:**
> "Good" | "Strong" | "Cool"

**Good traits:**
> "Vengeful" | "Methodical" | "Uncompromising"

**Tips:**
- These are EXTERNAL traits‚Äîwhat others SEE
- Make them interesting (flaws are good!)
- They should inform how others interact with your character
- Think: "If someone met my character, what 3 words would they use?"
